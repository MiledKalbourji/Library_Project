Welcome to My Library Project!

Over the last 4 months, I have explored the patterns of students coming in and out of Addams Library. The data highlights the library's importance as a hub for learning and relaxation.

Point of the Project:

Using hard data, this project aims to showcase the recovery of Addams Library post-2020 and its value for students and the public alike.
